Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

CheckMyContract ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use CheckMyContract.co.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use our service:

  • Email address — collected when you enter it to access the contract review tool
  • Uploaded contract files — documents you upload for analysis (PDF, DOC, DOCX, TXT)
  • Usage data — how many contract checks you have used in the current month

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and operate the contract review service
  • Send you your analysis results and service-related emails
  • Enforce the monthly free check limit per user
  • Improve and maintain the service

Your Contract Documents

Contract files you upload are sent to Anthropic's Claude API for analysis and are not permanently stored on our servers. We do not use your contract documents to train AI models. Your documents are processed solely for the purpose of generating your analysis report.

Email Communications

By entering your email address, you agree to receive your contract analysis report and occasional service-related emails from CheckMyContract. We will never send you spam or sell your email address to third parties. You can unsubscribe at any time by contacting us.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate CheckMyContract:

  • Anthropic Claude API — for AI-powered contract analysis
  • Supabase — for storing email addresses and usage data
  • Resend — for sending email reports
  • Vercel — for hosting and serving the application

Each of these services has their own privacy policies governing how they handle data.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. Your data is stored securely and access is restricted. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Retention

We retain your email address and usage data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of email communications at any time

Children's Privacy

CheckMyContract is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us at hello@checkmycontract.co.
