FAQ

Common questions.

Everything you need to know before you upload your first contract.

Is this legal advice?

No. CheckMyContract is an AI-powered informational tool. It helps you understand your contract in plain English but does not constitute legal advice. For high-stakes contracts, always consult a licensed attorney.

What file types do you support?

We support PDF, DOC, DOCX, and TXT files. Most contracts sent by clients will be in one of these formats.

Is my contract kept private?

Yes. Your document is transmitted securely and is not stored longer than necessary for analysis. We do not use your contracts to train AI models or share them with any third parties.

How accurate is the analysis?

Our AI uses advanced language models and is well-suited for identifying common contract risks in freelance agreements. That said, no AI is perfect — we recommend using it as a first pass before negotiating or signing.

What kinds of contracts can I review?

CheckMyContract works best with freelance service agreements, client contracts, NDAs, independent contractor agreements, and consulting contracts. It's built specifically with freelancers in mind.

How does the free plan work?

You get 1 free contract review per month, no credit card required. Just enter your email and upload your contract. Your free check resets on the 1st of every month.
